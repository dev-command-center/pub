# PUNNARAJ Succession Boundary

PUNNARAJ may use third-party platforms, but PUNNARAJ must not be governed by third-party platforms.

A third-party account is a convenience, not the inheritance root.

PUNNARAJ succession must be supported by:

- ZERO evidence
- local/exported repository copies
- documented recovery procedure
- key inventory under controlled access
- human recognition inside the family line
- public surfaces that explain responsibility without exposing private evidence

## Operational accounts

GitHub, Cloudflare, Apple, Google, OpenAI, and other services may remain useful operational handles.

They can provide access to their own platforms.

They do not decide the legitimacy of PUNNARAJ succession.

## Practical succession

Future heirs should receive access through documented roles and recovery procedures, not by depending only on one third-party login.

Where possible:

- project assets should live in organizations or documented repositories
- actions should be attributable to individual user accounts
- local copies should exist outside vendor systems
- critical evidence must remain under ZERO control
