# AI-Assisted Publication Principle

AI may help produce material for punnaraj.com.

AI may help:

- draft text
- organize public pages
- generate code
- summarize internal context into public-safe language
- prepare website updates

AI is not the publisher.

Publication on this domain is a human-authorized act. If material is published on this domain, the domain owner accepts responsibility for that publication.

The correct response to error is not to blame AI. The correct response is to receive the report, review the evidence, correct or clarify the issue, record the revision, and improve the process.

## Public rule

This site may contain AI-assisted writing, code, or generated artifacts.

All published material on this domain is human-authorized under the responsibility of the domain owner.

AI is a tool in the production process, not an independent legal or moral publisher.
