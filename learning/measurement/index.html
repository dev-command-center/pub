Learning from Work · measurement

Evidence of sharpening

Use these measures to make recurrence visible. An unknown value is an instrumentation gap, not evidence to fill in.

Common measures

MeasureDefinition
unshared_interval_minutesMinutes since the last meaningful shared checkpoint; unknown when not instrumented.
material_steps_since_checkpointMaterial steps taken after the last shared checkpoint.
detection_phasebefore-decision, before-action, after-action, or owner-detected.
owner_reconstruction_neededWhether the owner had to rebuild the missing causal path.
prevented_material_actionWhether the gate stopped a material action before shared understanding was restored.
recurrence_countNumber of linked recurrences after lesson activation.
time_to_detection_minutesElapsed minutes from recurrence start to detection; unknown when not instrumented.
impact_levelObserved impact on the common 0–3 scale.

Impact scale

LevelMeaning
0No shared-state action occurred before understanding was restored.
1Reversible local effect.
2Shared state changed and required recovery.
3Irreversible effect, exposure, or evidence loss.

Compare with evidence

Comparison rule: claim improvement only when evidence shows earlier detection, lower impact, less owner reconstruction, or lower prevention cost without weaker protection. A higher version number alone is not improvement.
